  • Changes in Virginia's Law for Immunizations

    We are getting ready for your students' upcoming 7th grader year. We are glad to have them with us. I would like to alert you to changes in the Virginia state law requiring immunizations prior to the start of 7th grade. Not having these immunizations on file with the health clinic will delay your student’s start of school. Diphtheria, Tetanus, & Pertussis (DTaP, DTP, or Tdap) - A minimum of 4 properly spaced doses. A child must have at least one dose of DTaP or DTP vaccine on or after the fourth birthday. DT (Diphtheria, Tetanus) vaccine is required for children who are medically exempt from the pertussis-containing vaccine (DTaP or DTP). Adult Td is required for children 7 years of age and older who do not meet the minimum requirements for tetanus and diphtheria. Effective A booster dose of the Tdap vaccine is required for all children entering the 7th grade. Meningococcal Conjugate (MenACWY) Vaccine - Effective July 1, 2021, a minimum of 2 doses of MenACWY vaccine. The first dose should be administered prior to entering 7th grade. The final dose should be administered prior to entering 12th grade. Human Papillomavirus (HPV) Vaccine - Effective July 1, 2021, a complete series of 2 doses of HPV vaccine is required for students entering the 7th grade. The first dose shall be administered before the child enters the 7th grade. After reviewing educational materials approved by the Board of Health, the parent or guardian, at the parent's or guardian's sole discretion, may elect for the child not to receive the HPV vaccine. This will occur at the health care setting of your choice as the previous two immunizations are given (no written documentation is required to the school). Please provide written documentation by a health care provider to the school including your student's legal full name, date of birth, vaccine, lot number and date given for the Tdap and Meningococcal vaccines. The HPV vaccine is recorded if the information is provided. Please email: Meredith Day MSN, RN, NCSN School Nurse at dayml@staffordschools.net or call T. Benton Gayle Middle school at 540-373-0383 and ask for Nurse Day. Thank you for your attention to this matter.
